Abbreviation/Acronym Key

G Game The number of games in which a player has appeared.
AB At-Bat The number of official at-bats by a batter.
R Run The number of times a baserunner safely reaches home plate.
H Hit The number of times a batter hits the ball and reaches base safely without aid of an error or fielder's choice.
2B Double The number of times a batter his the ball and reaches second base without aid of an error or fielder's choice.
3B Triple The number of times a batter his the ball and reaches third base without aid of an error or fielder's choice.
HR Home Run The number of times a batter hits the ball and reaches home plate scoring a run either by hitting the ball out of play in fair territory or without aid of an error or fielder's choice.
TB Total Base The number of bases that a batter reaches through his hits without aid of an error or fielder's choice.
RBI Run Batted In The number of runs that score safely due to a batter hitting a ball.
K Strikeout The number of strikeouts by a batter.
AVG Average The average number of hits by a batter definited by hits divided by at-bats (H/AB).
SLG Slugging The measure of the power of a hitter, total bases divided by at-bats (TB/AB).

Official Rulebook

Games

1. All games will be played at Horn (except for the All-Star game).

2. Each game will be 6 innings long (unless there are extra innings).

3. Pitchers cannot start back to back games (not including the All-Star game).

4. Two step lead on every base (runner can be called or tagged out).

5. Bunting is allowed as long as it doesn't bounce more than twice before reaching the pitcher's mound.

Ground Rules

1. Errors are given to throws that travel out of the cage. If the ball hits the pitcher's hand, it's an error on the pitcher, otherwise it's an error on the thrower.

2. If a ball gets stuck in the trailer steps, it's a single for the closest one and a double for the farthest one.

3. If a ball goes in and out of the fire escape then it's an automatic single.

4. If the ball hits the window on the roof, it's a home run, but if it hits the chimney then it's a live ball.

5. If a ball gets stuck on the roof then it is still considered a home run, not an out.

Rosters

1. Rosters are drafted to consist of 4 players on each team and all 4 players are expected to play every game.

2. There will be no replacing players unless it is necessary and the league will find the replacement.

3. Trades are allowed as long as there's an even player amount being traded to both teams.

Playoffs

1. All four teams will make the playoffs.

2. The first seed will have two byes into the championship. The second seed will have one bye to play the winner of the third and fourth team in the first round.

3. All rounds will be single elimination except for the championship which will be best of 3.

All-Star/MVP

1. All-Star Game and Home Run Derby will be played at Holy Innocents.

2. All-Stars will be decied through a league leader point system at the end of the season.

3. MVP will be decided by the All-Star league leader point system as well as with player voting (all players must vote for someone else).

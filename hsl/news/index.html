League News

2003 HSL Champions

Goodnight Irenes defeated the #1 seeded Shark Attack in the first two games of the best of three series to become the 2003 HSL League Champions.

League MVP Goes To...

Dave (Goodnight Irenes) voted the 2003 HSL League MVP with Jon (Bomb Squad) coming in a close second and Danny (Indian Burners) in third.

Home Run King's All-Star Performance

East beat the West, 15-10, with help from 4 home runs by Jon (Bomb Squad), the All-Star MVP.

Home Run Derby Dominance

Jon (Bomb Squad) defeated Danny (Indian Burners), 8-4, in the championship round.

Top Two Seeds in Championship

Goodnight Irenes defeated the Bomb Squad, 22-13, in the second round wildcard to face Shark Attack in the championship.

Goodnight Irenes Has Opponent

Bomb Squad defeated the Indian Burners, 15-9, in the first wildcard round to face Goodnight Irenes in the second wildcard round.

Top Seed has 3 of 8 Announced All-Stars

Dave & Hink (Goodnight Irenes), Danny & Kev (Indian Burners), Jon (Bomb Squad) and Aaron, Mark & Shark (Shark Attack) named 2003 HSL All-Stars.

HR Derby Includes Top 4 HR Hitters

Hink (Goodnight Irenes), Jon (Bomb Squad), Danny and Mike (Indian Burners) announced as participants in the Home Run Derby as the top 4 home run hitters.

Playoff Matchups Set

Goodnight Irenes clinched the second seed by defeating the Bomb Squad, 24-18, leaving the Bomb Squad and Indian Burners to face off in the first wildcard game.

Top Seed Clinched

Shark Attack clinched the top seed of the playoffs after a 14-11 game 7 victory over the Bomb Squad.

Bomb Squad/Indian Burners Trade

Bomb Squad traded Mike to the Indian Burners for Jer after both teams lost their first game.
